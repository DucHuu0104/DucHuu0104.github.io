Who am I?

I am an undergraduate student in Artificial Intelligence at FPT University and currently a Researcher at AiTA Lab.

My research interests focus on Machine Learning and Deep Learning, particularly in EEG signal analysis and Computer Vision. I am interested in developing robust models that can learn meaningful representations from complex data such as brain signals and visual information.

I also have experience building automation tools and AI-driven systems to support data processing and research workflows.

In research, I am the first author of a watermarking paper presented at a national conference and have contributed as a co-author to several research papers submitted to international conferences.
